About Invest in Markham

The goal is to build a "Global Markham" – to connect Canada's high tech capital with the world. Actions taken will include fortifying international relations in economic development, attracting foreign investment, and attracting new opportunities through Markham's multilingual character.

Statistics

Markham is composed of a highly educated workforce and the most innovative companies.

80% of the population in Markham identify as a visible minority. The different cultures and varying points of view in the workplace allow for an increase in creativity and innovation for businesses located in Canada.

Markham is home to over 1500 technology and science companies. It has become a centre for innovation and overall, a favourable location for foreign investment.

Over 60% of the population in Markham have a postsecondary education. The strong educational background amongst the population introduces a great opportunity for foreign investment.
